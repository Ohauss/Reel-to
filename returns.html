Returns & refunds

We want you to be thrilled with your new purchase. However, if you need to return an item, we're here to help. This policy outlines how and when you can return products.

1.Returns information

You have the right to return purchased items in accordance with our returns policy:

  • To initiate a return,contact us via email ..
  • Before returning the item, please follow our packaging guidelines to ensure it arrives safely and securely.
  • A summary of the key terms is provided on this page for your convenience. For full details of our Returns Policy, please refer to our Terms & Conditions page.

2. Returns of unwanted items

We gladly accept returns for online purchases within 30 days for a refund or exchange, as part of our extended returns policy. To qualify for a refund, the returned item must meet the following criteria:

  • It must be in original condition, unused, and without cosmetic imperfections (scratches, chips, stains, etc.).
  • It must come in its original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and peripherals.
  • Not have faults caused by improper use.
  • Comply with legal obligations

If any of these conditions are not met, we reserve the right to deduct a reasonable amount from the refund to reflect any loss in value. Please note that unless the return shipment has been arranged by the customer or unless the item is faulty, a $20 per-box fee will be applied to all returned items which covers courier collection and return delivery. For returns of items shipped on a pallet, the return cost will be determined based on the shipment's weight and size.

* Please note that throughout the return process it is the customer's responsibility to ensure the items arrive safely and undamaged to our returns address. This means that you are accountable for the condition of the item and any damage that may occur in transit, including the cases when courier collection is arranged by us. We therefore strongly recommend that you take extra care when packaging items, ensuring they are as securely packaged as possible.

3. Returns of faulty items

In accordance with the Consumer Rights Act of 2015, any item that arrives faulty or develops a fault within a specified period of time is eligible for a return or replacement, providing the damage has not been caused by accident, negligence or misuse on behalf of the customer.

If the product develops a fault within 30 days of purchase you may request a replacement or a full refund.

If a fault develops outside the initial 30-day period but within 6 months, you have the right to a free repair. If the repair is not possible or unsuccessful, you are entitled to a replacement or a refund. For any faults that developed beyond the first 6 months of ownership, please contact us to discuss your options.

The cost of return shipment of any faulty items will be covered by us.

4. Order cancellation

If the product has not been dispatched yet, we can cancel your order right away. Please contact us via email at Email: support@reeltoreal.store or call us on Phone: +44 20 1234 5678.

If the product has already been dispatched but hasn't been delivered yet we may be able to cancel and reverse the delivery, depending on the shipping method used.

Please note, the refund will be processed only once the item has reached our warehouse.

5. Item inspection and Refunds

Once the returned item has been received it will be inspected by our warehouse team as quickly as possible. The refund can only be processed after a full inspection of the goods has been completed.

Please note that we reserve the right to deduct an appropriate amount from the refund to reflect any reduction in the value of the goods, in case it has been caused by mishandling of the item or incorrect packaging.

All refunds are issued to the original payment method used by the buyer and can take up to 7 working days to appear on their account.

6. Exclusions to returns policy

Please note that Distance Selling regulations do not apply to bespoke, unique, custom or special order items. These items are specifically ordered or customised based on customer requests and cannot be restocked. We encourage customers to review their orders carefully before finalising their purchase. If you have any questions about whether an item qualifies as a special order, please contact our customer service team prior to placing your order.

Please also note that products that have a direct contact with the ear can only be returned for a full refund if sent back with hygiene seals unbroken and in unused condition.

7. Responsibility for returned items

When returning items to us, please be aware that the responsibility for the goods remains with you until they are safely received at our warehouse. This applies to all returns, including those where we facilitate courier collection on your behalf. It is crucial to ensure that the items are well-protected and securely packaged to prevent any damage during transit. Your accountability extends to maintaining the condition of the items and safeguarding them against potential damages while they are in transit.

In the case of unwanted items being returned, if the item arrives at our warehouse in a damaged state due to inadequate packaging, we reserve the right to deduct a corresponding amount from your refund. This deduction is to account for the loss in the item’s value or the cost associated with restoring it to a sellable condition. It is essential to package the item properly to avoid such deductions.

If you are returning a faulty item and it sustains additional damage during transit due to improper packaging, we will exercise our right to charge you for the repair of the item. This deduction will cover the loss of value and the cost of repairs necessitated by the additional damage. To prevent such situations, ensure that the item is securely and adequately packaged before returning it to us.

This policy does not affect your statutory rights.

If you have any questions about returns & refunds, please don't hesitate to contact us.
